Have you heard the news?

Commander Wilf on his Klingoff ancestry

Cadet Bank has recently on the orders of the high council gone to the Klingoff home world to interview the very famous Commander Wilf about his troubling family life. Until now Wilf has been a warrior of honour, that to rival the mightiness of the house of DumarC in all their glory. His father's sins in his past life betraying the Klingoff home world is of no secret and Cadet Bank was eager to see how Wilf was doing on his sabbatical away from the Council with his son Antonin.

Captain Pickle and his commendation

From ensign to captain in less than six years, captain Pickle has yet to astonish the cosmos yet with his remarkable edge for drinking tea all the time and pulling his shirt down. Will he ever regain his true french accent? The council have decided to award Captain Pickle with a commendation for having a nice bald head that is kept nice and shiny and polished. It has become a true wonder on the Academy campus to see a glancing glow in the distance and there is a comfort to be had in knowing that Captain Pickle will always habe your back.

Lieutenant Byte finds a new brother

Robots can dream, eat, produce electricity and also take in electricity. So do robots really dream of electric dreams? Lieutenant Byte answers this for us as his memory circuits have shorted and have revealed a hidden subroutine . Could this mean that his unknown creator has left a trail to find him? Byte has already discovered he has an identical brother, a fellow named Nibble who prefers to reside in a hole like a hamster, eating only with 4 bits of food at a time.
