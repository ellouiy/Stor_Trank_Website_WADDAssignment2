No Win Scenario

A Classic Stor Trank Game

Welcome to the Unwinnable Game

The Klingoffs are attacking your ship. You have no firepower left and all you have is a giant shield to deflect the hits. BUT the shield doesn’t cover your entire station. Move the paddle using your mouse to rotate the shields to deflect any oncoming Klingoffs. If a Klingoff ship creeps up behind you, they can entire your safety bubble and beam out your crew. When you reach a missing persons number by 1000, you have failed in your endeavour to save the station, you have too few crew to man it and you will be boarded and captured within seconds. Try and last the longest without hitting your missing persons limit.

You’ll feel defeated, and indeed you shall be.

This is a no win scenario.
